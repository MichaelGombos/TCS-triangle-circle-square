Welcome to the TCS game.

This game is a turn based combination game where to goal is to remember your opponenets starting deck to narrow down their possible moves.

GAME RULES

  • Each player will be given a random hand of 12 cards
  • Square cards beat circle cards, circle cards beat triangle, and triangles beat squares
  • Each player can only play 3 cards on each turn
  • The first player with 5 points will win the game.
